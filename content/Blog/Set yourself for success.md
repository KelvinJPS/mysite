To set up yourself for success, you have get into doing things where you win even when you fail.
The biggest problem when starting anything on  your own, it's motivation, when you don't have a boss or professor to set objectives and  a framework. You're more likely to abandon your goals and fail.

The reason it's that we as humans think a lot about opportunity cost.
Opportunity cost is ==the value of the best alternative that's sacrificed when a decision is made==.
When we are in school, university or a job most of the desicions are taken by us and doing something in your own like self studying means not gaining a certification or studying something that won't led to anything.
Starting a business versus getting a  job where in the job the money is secured, and stable but when working for yourself means not making any money or even worse loosing money.
We have fear of  failure. 
What we can do to alleviate it? 

The solution it's to reduce or even remove the possibility of failure.
And how we can do it?

I'll give you examples of studying on your own and starting a business.

## Starting a business
 
 If you build a business you'll learn many skills like in tech marketing, administration, time management, project management and more.
