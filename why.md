## The purpose of this website

Sometimes, I feel like I have been wasting my time these last few years, and the  time keeps passing 
But still, I have nothing to show for it, the reason for this website is that I want it  to be a place where
I can put the stuff I build and learn, so in the future I can look back and say, yeah, I did this, this is what I have been investing my time in.
